Top 3 Inspiring Photographers


Chase Jarvis

Chase Jarvis was originally planning on attending medical school, but after his grandfather passed away and left him his photography equipment he discovered his true passion. After studying at the University of Washington Chase Jarvis has been quite successful in the commercial photography business. He has been recognized for multiple awards, including an award from the Advertising Photographers of America.


Sarah Ann Loreth

Sarah Ann Loreth began her photography career as a hobby. Using photography as an outlet for stress and as a way to relax from a demanding job. However she soon realized that what she really wanted to do was travel and capture powerful images of the natural world. Sarah's main focus is on travel photography and landscapes.


Cedric Blanchon

Cedric Blanchon is a French photographer known mainly for his use of an iphone camera for his work. He creates stunning and visually compelling works that often use some kind of illusion or method to convey some unusual photos. He describes his work as iphotography and shoots most of his work on the iphone 4 that he carries with him wherever he goes.
